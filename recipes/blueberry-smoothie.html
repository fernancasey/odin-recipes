Blueberry Bananas Smoothie

Description

Wild blueberries are sweet and nutritious. With TWICE the antioxidants as ordinary blueberries. Wild blueberries also have especially rich in anthocyanins, this gives the blueberries their deep blue-purple color. The frozen bananas provide extra sweetness without the need of added sugars!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen wild blueberries
  • 1 cup frozen bananas
  • 2/3 - 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a high-speed blender. Start with 2/3 cup of milk and add more as needed.
  2. Blend on high until you reach desired consistency.
  3. Serve immediately.
