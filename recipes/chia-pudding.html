Vanilla Chia Pudding

Description

This chia pudding recipe made with coconut milk (or your choice of plant milk), chia seeds, and liquid sweetener is a perfect, quick, and creamy recipe, and you only need ingredients. Eat as is, or top with, granola, cacao nibs, coconut shreds, or your choice of fruit. It's an easy, make-ahead gluten-free, dairy-free breakfast idea for a busy week or try it for a high protein, high fiber, omega-3 filling snack!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp Chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp Maple syrup
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into mason jars (or your glass jar of choice) and stir mixture together.
  2. Allow the mixture to sit for 1 minute. The chia seeds will begin to settle at the bottom as they expand.
  3. Stir well again; keep in mind that strring well a few times will help prevent lumps from forming.
  4. Refrigerate for 4 hours or leave in the refrigerator overnight.
